ALIENWARE 17
Subtlety has never been Alienware's strong suit, but with this iteration of the brand's 17-inch gaming laptop, it's what you don't see that deserves the biggest praise. This time around, the Alienware 17 has Nvidia's GeForce GTX 980M GPU under the hood, giving the system a serious graphics boost.
But if Nvidia's top mobile GPU doesn't satisfy your frame-rate lust, you can unlock even more power with Alienware's optional graphics amplifier, which, when paired with a desktop GPU, unlocks a whole new level of kick-ass.
